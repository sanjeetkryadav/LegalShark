Creditors do have the right to sue for unpaid debts. However, having legal representation drastically reduces this risk. If they do file a lawsuit, our attorneys are equipped to defend you in court, often forcing a favorable settlement before trial.

Debt settlement typically requires accounts to go into default, which does temporarily lower your score. However, eliminating massive debt lowers your debt-to-income ratio. Once settled, we provide guidance on aggressively rebuilding your credit profile.
